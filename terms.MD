MorphoCloud does not provide the security controls necessary for Human Subject Research.

* **Strict Prohibition:** Usage of MorphoCloud for any "Human Subject Research," as defined by the NIH under 45 CFR 46, is strictly prohibited.
* **User Responsibility:** It is the user's sole responsibility to ensure their data is fully de-identified and does not meet the legal threshold for HSR before uploading it to the Service.
